Here are the general instructions about the game:

  • The player needs to catch as many eggs as possible from the sky.
  • Player has five lives. As soon as the player fails to catch the egg for 5 times, the game ends there.
  • Score will be displayed at the top right corner of your screen.
  • When score reaches a multiple of 10, the level will increase which will increase the speed of the eggs
  • To catch the egg: move the player and place it appropriately so that the egg falls inside the player's basket.
  • Use mouse controls to move the player
